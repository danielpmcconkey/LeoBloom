**Key design decisions this session:**

1. **Mortgage payments are transfers, not expenses.** Both Lockhart
   (Rocket Mortgage) and personal (SECU) post as liability reductions:
   - Lockhart: Dr 2110 (Mortgage Payable — Lockhart), Cr 1110 (CMA)
   - Personal: Dr 2210 (Mortgage Payable — Personal), Cr 1210 (Brokerage)
   - Interest/tax/insurance breakdown happens at year-end via 1098.
   - Same treatment as the auto loan (Dr 2230, Cr cash).

2. **Brokerage ↔ CMA transfers are owner equity moves:**
   - Money into CMA: Dr 1110, Cr 3010 (Owner's Investment)
   - Money out of CMA: Dr 3020 (Owner's Draws), Cr 1110
   - Only CMA leg posts — brokerage leg skipped.

3. **Pre-CMA mortgage payments (Churchill):** Dan may have paid the
   Lockhart mortgage from SECU checking before the CMA was set up.
   Treatment: Dr 2110, Cr 3010 (Owner's Investment — personal funds
   used for business). Will surface during SECU import.

4. **Composite key date format:** `YYYYMMDD` (no dashes). The manual
   test transaction used this format. Parser was generating `YYYY-MM-DD`
   — caught by the dedup test, fixed, re-parsed.
