### Dan reviewed every finding and made decisions
Key decisions documented in conversation:

1. **Multi-transaction atomicity** — go with idempotency guards (cheap fix). Check for existing journal entry by reference_type/reference_value before posting.
2. **Database indexes** — approved. PostgreSQL doesn't index FK columns automatically (Dan thought it did). One migration.
3. **CHECK constraints** — declined. No business logic in DB layer. ADR written.
4. **Domain-based project reorg** — approved. Move domain-specific services/repos from Utilities into LeoBloom.Ledger and LeoBloom.Ops. Utilities stays as generic infrastructure only. This is NOT the layer-based split the reviewers proposed. ADR written.
5. **Error type schism** (Result<T, string> vs Result<T, string list>) — keep as-is. The difference reflects a real business distinction (single vs multiple failure modes). Not worth "fixing."
6. **Utilities split by layer** — declined. ADR says domain-based split instead.
7. **Empty password hard fail** — declined. Not worth it for single-user tool.
8. **IncludeErrorDetail** — move from hardcoded in DataSource.fs to appsettings.json connection string.
