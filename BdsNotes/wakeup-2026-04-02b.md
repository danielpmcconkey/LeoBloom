### Key decisions made this session

- **Migration tool: Migrondi** — F#-native, chosen over DbUp (C#-flavored) and
  Evolve (dead). SQL files are portable; switching runners is trivial.
- **No CHECK constraints** — 3NF with FK constraints to lookup tables suffices.
  Dan's explicit ruling.
- **No DB-level unique on `(obligation_agreement_id, expected_date)`** — business
  logic, stays in app layer. Dan's explicit ruling.
- **ON DELETE RESTRICT on all FKs** — append-only ledger + soft deletes everywhere.
- **Migrondi journal table lives in `migrondi` schema** — the `claude` role can't
  write to `public` (Postgres 15+ default). Domain data in `ledger`/`ops`, tooling
  metadata in `migrondi`. Dan confirmed he never uses `public` schema.
